# Faith-Integrated Psychological First Aid (PFA) Treatment Plan

## Introduction
This template provides a structured yet flexible framework for delivering Psychological First Aid (PFA) to individuals and families affected by disaster or trauma, with intentional integration of faith-based resources and cultural competence. It draws on the NCTSN PFA model, cultural competence principles, and ethical guidelines for faith integration in clinical practice.
